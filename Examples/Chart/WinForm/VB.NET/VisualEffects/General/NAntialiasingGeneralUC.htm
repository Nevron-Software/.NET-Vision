Nevron Chart for .NET can leverage the OpenGL capabilities to render antialiased lines and points. Antialiasing greatly improves the quality of the generated chart, because it removes the jagged lines. Note however that it slows down the rendering.
Enable antialiasing - turns on and off the antialiasing feature of the control.
Bar style - changes the style of the bar series.
The Width and Depth scroll bars control the dimensions of the bars relative to the chart floor.
Lights - turns on and off lighting.
