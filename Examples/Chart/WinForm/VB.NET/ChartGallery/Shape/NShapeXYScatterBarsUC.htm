This example demonstrates how to use the Shape Series to display XY scatter bars.
You can change the fill styles applied to the bars by pressing the Bar Fill Style buttons.
The Inflate Margins check fits the bars in the Chart Area.
The Axes Round To Tick check makes the PrimaryY and PrimaryX axes begin and end on exact tick marks.
The shape of the bars is controlled by the Bar Shape combo.
The Change X Values button changes the X positions of the bars.
