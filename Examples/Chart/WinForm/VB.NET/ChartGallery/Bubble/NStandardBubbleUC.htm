This example demonstrates a standard Bubble chart.
From the Bubble Shape combo you can control the shape of the bubbles.
The Min Bubble Size scroll controls the size of the smallest bubble.
The Max Bubble Size scroll controls the size of the largest bubble.
When you check the Different Colors check the bubbles will be displayed in different colors. Otherwise the filling of the bubbles can be edited by pressing the Bubble Fill Style button.
The Legend Format combo controls the information displayed in the legend.
