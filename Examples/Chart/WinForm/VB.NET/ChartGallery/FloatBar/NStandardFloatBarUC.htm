This example demonstrates a standard Float Bar chart.
From the Bar Style Combo you can control the shape of the bars.
The Width % scroll controls the width of the bars in percents of the floor grid cell width.
The Depth % scroll controls the depth of the bars in percents of the floor grid cell depth.
When you check the Different Colors check the bars will be displayed in different colors.
Otherwise the filling of the bars can be edited by pressing the Bar Fill Style button.
