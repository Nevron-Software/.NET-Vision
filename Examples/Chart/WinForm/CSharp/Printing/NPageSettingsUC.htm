Nevron Chart for .NET has built-in print support. This feature can help you add printing capabilities to your .NET applications in minutes.
Installed printers - enumerates the printers installed on the machine.
Paper group - controls the paper size and source.
Margins group - the margins in inches used by the control when printing.
Settings group - controls the page orientation (landscape or portrait), grayscaling and the settings used to internally scale the image when printing in order to leverage the better resolution of the printer than the screen.
Print button - prints a chart on the selected printer.
Built-in print manager buttons - shows the built-in print manager.
