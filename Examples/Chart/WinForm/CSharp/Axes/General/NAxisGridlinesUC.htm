This example demonstrates the capabilities of the axis gridlines. Major gridlines are displayed at axis major tick marks values. Minor gridlines are displayed at axis minor tick marks values.

The gridlines can be displayed on several visible chart walls. In this example you can show the gridlines of the left axis on the left and back chart walls. The gridlines of the Bottom axis can be displayed on the bottom and back walls, while the gridlines of the Depth axis can be displayed on the floor and left walls.

The example demonstrates left axis minor gridlines displayed on the left and (or) back chart walls.

You can control the color and style of the gridlines.
