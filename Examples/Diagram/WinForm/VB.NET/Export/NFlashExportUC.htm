This example demonstrates the built in support for Flash (SWF) export, which is implemented by the NFlashExporter extension. When you click on the <Export to SWF> button a SWF file is generated and saved in the application's startup directory. The file is then opened with your default Flash player.

Exporting to Flash is as easy as writing 2 lines of code:

NFlashExporter exporter = new NFlashExporter(document);
exporter.SaveToFile(fileName);
