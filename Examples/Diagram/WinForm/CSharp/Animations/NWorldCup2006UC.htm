This example demonstrates the creation of a tournament diagram and makes use of the following features:

  1. Table shapes are used to hold the information for the teams, their flags and the scores.
  2. Routable connectors are used to connect the matches from the different stages of the tournament.
  3. A layered graph layout with left to right direction is finally applied to layout the diagram in the best human readable way.
  4. The shapes of the diagram have preset animations styles.

When you click on the Generate SWF or the Generate XAML button a movie (in the corresponding format) is generated and saved in the application's startup directory. The file is then opened with the default viewer for the generated movie.
