This example demonstrates how to create a custom 2D shape. It also demonstrates the ability to define ports, which are anchored to the primitives used to build the shape.

In this example the coffee cup consists of three parts - cup, handle and steam. They all have different fill style, but inherit the shadow style of the document.

The coffee cup has two ports:

  1. Dynamic port anchored to the cup part of the shape. Note than the 1D shapes connecting the shapes glue to the contour of the cup.
  2. Rotated bounds port anchored to the middle right side of the handle.
